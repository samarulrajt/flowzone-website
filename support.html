How do I sync my data across devices?
Simply sign in with the same account on all your devices. Your focus sessions will automatically sync.
What does Premium include?
Premium unlocks ad-free experience, unlimited custom categories, advanced statistics, and priority support.
How do I restore my Premium purchase?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Premium ‚Üí Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Can I export my data?
Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Export Data to download your focus session history as a CSV file.
How does the leaderboard work?
The leaderboard ranks users based on total focus time. You can view rankings for daily, weekly, monthly, or all-time periods.
How do I delete my account?
Contact us at support@flowzone.app and we'll process your account deletion request within 48 hours.
